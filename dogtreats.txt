Cheese biscuits

Ingredients
- 100g cottage cheese
- 70g gouda
- 30g parmesan cheese
- 1 egg
- 100g potato flour
- 50 ml water


1. Mix cottage cheese, gouda, parmesan cheese and egg using a blender. Add potato flour and mix it again. Add water step by step.

2. Fill mixture in Form

3. Bake mixture in the oven for 20 minutes at 180Â°C.
